About The Event

Organized by BIS Standards Club, VIT Vellore

48 Hours Challenge

A 48-hour hackathon (2 days, 2 nights) held at Anna Auditorium, VIT Vellore as part of Gravitas 2025.

Team Participation

Open to all VITians. Form teams of 3-5 members and register through VTOP.

Objectives

Promote awareness of Indian Standards, foster interdisciplinary teamwork, and encourage tech-based compliance and safety.

Innovation Tracks

Choose your domain and innovate with BIS standards

Smart Infrastructure & Urban Tech

Use BIS civil/structural codes like IS 875 & IS 16700 to build smart, compliant infrastructure tools.

Healthcare & Biomedical Standards

Medical devices, sanitation & hygiene tech using standards like IS 13450, IS 15700.

Environmental Sustainability

Innovations around water, air, waste, and green systems – aligned with IS 10500, IS 5182.

Consumer Safety & Product Quality

Verify labels, test product safety, or create alert systems using standards like IS 15700.

Energy & Electrification

Battery, LED, solar system innovations with standards like IS 16046 and IS 16102.

Digital Systems & Cybersecurity

AI ethics, secure systems & data compliance based on IS 17428 & ISO/IEC standards.

Open Innovation (Wildcard)

For impactful ideas that don't fit a single domain but align with BIS principles.

Mechanical Systems & Manufacturing Standards

Innovations in manufacturing, design, and automation based on IS 210, IS 2062, IS 10218, and other mechanical standards.
